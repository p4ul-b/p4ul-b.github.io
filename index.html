Paul Barrow
Project Manager, Business Analyst and Product Manager

I have over 20 years of experience in pharmacovigilance from multiple perspective, from the UK medicines regulator, pharma industry and for software consultancies working with those clients.

Working at the junction between business, technical and quality teams, I thrive on understanding requirements, communicating needs and delivering solutions that deliver what users and customers need.

My drive to deliver combined with my knowledge of pharmacovigilance processes, regulations and technology solutions ensure customer success.
